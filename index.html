ABOUT US
Who Are We?
LaDebug is a female-driven club initiated by Hackoverflow Society. With over 300+ female students from over 60+ colleges across the country, the club was started with a motto of 'Debugging the gender gap'
Our Story
Our Vision
Work Ethics
A gap has always been observed among the genders in a professional domain, be it technical or non-technical. Karan Bhargav, an alumnus and the founder of Hackoverflow Society who witnessed this gap at our university decided to start an all-female club for the holistic growth of the female students from the ground level. The club started exclusively with the female students of Chandigarh University, but now we have over 300+ members from 60+ colleges across India.
